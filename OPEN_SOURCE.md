# Open Source And Claim Boundary

This Paper 9 skeleton is prepared for open research review. Code and tooling
are MIT licensed. Manuscripts, documentation, diagrams, and review packets are
CC BY 4.0 unless a file states otherwise.

The repository is non-promoting at creation. It records a downstream research
program and an upstream binding through Paper 8. It does not claim observed
particle catalog recovery, physical Standard Model content, physical particle
excitations, physical matter fields, physical gauge fields, physical quantum
dynamics, continuum quantum field theory, simulation-only promotion, fit-only
calibration, physical nature realization, or a unified field theory.
